🌱 Seeds Information

×

Rice Seeds

Uses: Main staple crop, high yield varieties

Season: Kharif (June-July)

Wheat Seeds

Uses: Flour production, high protein

Season: Rabi (Oct-Nov)

Cotton Seeds

Uses: Textile industry, oil production

Season: Kharif (May-June)

Maize Seeds

Uses: Food, animal feed, industrial

Season: Kharif & Rabi

Vegetable Seeds

Uses: Tomato, onion, potato cultivation

Season: Year-round

Pulses Seeds

Uses: Protein-rich food, soil enrichment

Season: Rabi (Oct-Nov)

💡 Tips:

  • Buy certified seeds from authorized dealers
  • Check seed germination rate before purchase
  • Store seeds in cool, dry place
  • Choose varieties suitable for your soil

🧪 Fertilizers Information

×

Urea (46% N)

Uses: Provides nitrogen for leaf growth and green color

Application: 50-100 kg per acre

DAP (18-46-0)

Uses: Rich in phosphorus, promotes root development

Application: 40-80 kg per acre

MOP (Potash)

Uses: Potassium for fruit quality and disease resistance

Application: 30-60 kg per acre

NPK (19-19-19)

Uses: Balanced nutrition for overall plant growth

Application: 50-100 kg per acre

Compost

Uses: Organic matter, improves soil structure

Application: 2-5 tons per acre

Vermicompost

Uses: Rich organic fertilizer, enhances soil fertility

Application: 1-2 tons per acre

Zinc Sulphate

Uses: Corrects zinc deficiency, improves crop yield

Application: 10-25 kg per acre

Calcium Nitrate

Uses: Provides calcium and nitrogen, prevents blossom end rot

Application: 20-40 kg per acre

Sulphur

Uses: Essential for protein synthesis, improves oil content

Application: 15-30 kg per acre

Boron

Uses: Improves flowering, fruit setting, and seed formation

Application: 5-10 kg per acre

💡 Tips:

  • Soil test before applying
  • Apply at right time
  • Mix organic & chemical
  • Avoid over-fertilization

🐛 Pesticides Information

×

Chlorpyrifos

Uses: Controls termites, aphids, and soil insects

Application: 400-500 ml per acre

Imidacloprid

Uses: Effective against sucking pests like whiteflies

Application: 100-150 ml per acre

Cypermethrin

Uses: Controls bollworms, caterpillars, and beetles

Application: 200-300 ml per acre

Neem Oil

Uses: Organic pesticide for various pests and diseases

Application: 1-2 liters per acre

Mancozeb

Uses: Fungicide for blight, rust, and leaf spots

Application: 400-600 gm per acre

Carbendazim

Uses: Broad-spectrum fungicide for seed treatment

Application: 200-250 gm per acre

Bacillus Thuringiensis

Uses: Biological pesticide for caterpillars and larvae

Application: 200-400 gm per acre

Profenofos

Uses: Controls bollworms, jassids, and thrips

Application: 400-500 ml per acre

Glyphosate

Uses: Herbicide for weed control in crops

Application: 1-1.5 liters per acre

Acetamiprid

Uses: Controls aphids, jassids, and whiteflies

Application: 60-80 gm per acre

⚠️ Safety:

  • Wear protective gear
  • Read label instructions
  • Spray early morning/evening
  • Keep away from children

💧 Irrigation Equipment

×

Drip Irrigation

Uses: Water-efficient system for row crops and orchards

Benefits: Saves 40-70% water, reduces weed growth

Sprinkler System

Uses: Suitable for all crops, especially vegetables

Benefits: Uniform water distribution, saves labor

Micro Sprinkler

Uses: Ideal for fruit trees and plantation crops

Benefits: Low pressure requirement, covers large area

Submersible Pump

Uses: Deep well water extraction for irrigation

Benefits: High efficiency, low maintenance

Centrifugal Pump

Uses: Surface water pumping for irrigation

Benefits: Cost-effective, easy to operate

Solar Pump

Uses: Eco-friendly irrigation using solar energy

Benefits: Zero electricity cost, environment friendly

PVC Pipes

Uses: Water distribution in irrigation systems

Benefits: Durable, corrosion-resistant, lightweight

Water Filter

Uses: Removes impurities from irrigation water

Benefits: Prevents clogging, extends system life

Irrigation Timer

Uses: Automated irrigation scheduling

Benefits: Saves time, ensures timely watering

Water Meter

Uses: Measures water usage in irrigation

Benefits: Helps optimize water consumption

💡 Tips:

  • Choose based on crop type
  • Regular maintenance
  • Use drip for water saving
  • Irrigate morning/evening

Government Schemes

PM-KISAN

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi - Direct income support of ₹6000/year

Eligibility:

  • All landholding farmers
  • Valid Aadhaar card

Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Records

PMFBY

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana - Crop insurance scheme

Eligibility:

  • All farmers growing notified crops
  • Sharecroppers & tenant farmers

Benefits:

  • Coverage for crop loss
  • Low premium rates

Soil Health Card

Get detailed soil analysis and recommendations

Eligibility:

  • All farmers
  • Free of cost

Benefits:

  • Soil nutrient status
  • Fertilizer recommendations
